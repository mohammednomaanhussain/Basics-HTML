FAQS

Welcome to our Airways Website FAQs! Here are some commonly asked questions and their answers to help you navigate through your air travel experience:


1. How can I book a flight ticket on your website?
To book a flight ticket, simply visit our website and enter your departure and arrival cities, travel dates, and the number of passengers. Our search engine will display available flights for you to choose from. Select your preferred flight, enter passenger details, and proceed to payment to confirm your booking.


2. What forms of payment do you accept?
We accept a variety of payment methods, including major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), debit cards, and online payment platforms. The available payment options will be displayed during the booking process.


3.How do I check-in for my flight?
You can check-in online through our website or mobile app. Enter your booking reference or e-ticket number, follow the prompts to select your seats and add any additional services, and receive your boarding pass electronically. You can also check-in at the airport counter on the day of your flight.
